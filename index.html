⚠️ What Changed Since the Book
🔴 Three Laws Changed — January 1, 2020
Life jacket now legally required. Min. 75N buoyancy per person on rivers (100N on Rhine). Standard 50N swim aids don't qualify. Children under 12 must wear one at all times. Fines CHF 50–140.
Alcohol limit abolished. The book's "0.5‰ rule" no longer applies to non-motorised inflatables. Must still be fit to navigate. Criminal liability if you cause an accident drunk.
Boat labeling now law. Name, address, and phone number must be visibly written on boat. Fine: CHF 40.

⚖️ Legal Rules 2026
Life jacket: Min. 75N per person. 100N on Rhine. Children under 12 must wear one. Fines CHF 50–140.
Boat labeling: Name, address, phone visibly written on boat. Fine: CHF 40.
Alcohol: No fixed limit for non-motorised inflatables. Must be fit to navigate.
Cannabis: Zero tolerance on Swiss waterways.

💡 Berner Fassmethode: Store boat in a 60L barrel (hardware store) on a folding luggage cart. Tow it behind the boat — keeps drinks cold AND saves space on board. 🍺

💡 Budget: Full kit (boat + paddles + pump + 2 life jackets + rope + bag) = ~CHF 290. Split 3–4 ways and you have it forever.
