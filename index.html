2026 updates

What changed since the book

The Salamander Verlag guidebook is still the bible — but three laws changed in 2020. Read before you go.

🔴 Three laws changed · January 2020
Don't get fined — read this
Life jackets now legally required. Min. 75N buoyancy per person on rivers (100N on the Rhine). Standard 50N swim aids don't qualify. Kids under 12 must wear one at all times. Fines CHF 50–140.
Alcohol limit abolished. The 0.5‰ rule no longer applies to non-motorised inflatables. You still have to be fit to navigate — criminal liability if you cause an accident drunk.
Boat labelling now mandatory. Name, address, and phone number must be visibly written on the boat. Fine: CHF 40.

🚫 Absolute don'ts
✗
Never paddle drunk (criminal liability)
✗
Never near weirs or hydro intakes
✗
Never in high water (Rhine > 1300 m³/s)
✗
Never alone on unfamiliar water
